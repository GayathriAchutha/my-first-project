About

Aswini Nachappa is a former Indian sprinter renowned for her performance in the 400 meters event. She represented India in several international competitions and later contributed to Indian athletics as a coach and sports administrator.

Career Highlights

  • Specialized in the 400m sprint
  • Represented India in Asian and international championships
  • Served as a coach and sports official after retirement
